# EHS Monthly Dashboard

## About the Project

This project is an **EHS (Environment ,Health, Safety) Monthly Dashboard** created to help organizations monitor their safety performance in a simple and visual way. It brings together important safety metrics like manpower, manhours, safety observations, training hours, and incident statistics into a single dashboard.

The goal is to make safety data easy to understand so that teams can identify risks, track improvements, and make better decisions.

## What the Dashboard Shows

* Total Manpower
* Total Manhours Worked
* Positive and Negative Safety Observations
* Training Hours Completed
* Unsafe Acts and Unsafe Conditions
* Lost Time Injury Frequency (LTIF)
* Severity Rate
* Company-wise Performance
* Safety Category Analysis

## Why This Project?

Managing safety data in spreadsheets can be difficult and time-consuming. This dashboard provides a clear overview of key HSE indicators, allowing managers and safety officers to quickly understand the current safety performance and focus on areas that need improvement.

## Features

* Clean and easy-to-read visualizations
* Monthly safety performance tracking
* Comparison of different companies or departments
* Analysis of unsafe acts and conditions
* Training statistics by topic
* Injury frequency and severity monitoring
